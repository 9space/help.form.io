--- title: Project Settings book: userguide chapter: projects slug: settings-project weight: 30 --- Users can configure the settings for their projects by clicking the Settings button within the PROJECT at the top right of the page. Project settings currently include: Email Settings, Roles, and Office 365 settings. This will be the home for all of your project settings in the future. - #### Email Settings <**form.io**> allows you the flexibility to use your existing email provider to manage your communications. Enter the email credentials for the email provider of your choice and click Save Settings when done. - #### Roles (see ROLES & PERMISSION chapter separately) - #### Office 365 (Settings to integrate with Outlook and other 365 applications)
