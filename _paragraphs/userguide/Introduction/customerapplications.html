Whenever you see the word APPLICATION used within this User Guide, it is refferring to the APPLICATION that developers are building outside of .

MAJOR COMPONENTS are built within the system, and are ultimately implemented within CUSTOMER APPLICATIONS. It is important to distinguish a CUSTOMER APPLICATION from the work being completing within .

When developers are working within to create the MAJOR COMPONENTS fortheir CUSTOMER APPLICATIONs, we refer to that work as PROJECTS.
