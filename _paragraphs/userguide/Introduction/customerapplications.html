--- title: Customer Applications book: userguide chapter: introduction slug: cutomer-applications weight: 7 --- Whenever you see the word APPLICATION used within this User Guide, it is referring to the APPLICATION that developers are building outside of <**form.io**>. <**form.io**> MAJOR COMPONENTS are built within the <**form.io**> system, and are ultimately implemented within CUSTOMER APPLICATIONS. It is important to distinguish a CUSTOMER APPLICATION from the work being completing within <**form.io**>. When developers are working within <**form.io**> to create the <**form.io**> MAJOR COMPONENTS for their CUSTOMER APPLICATIONs, we refer to that work as PROJECTS.
